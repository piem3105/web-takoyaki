Frequently Ask Question

Q: What's PRIMES TAKOYAKI?
A:Primes takoyaki is a traditional Japanese dish with historical significance.
It started as an efficient way to prepare a nutritious meal during wartime, then it evolved into a beloved dish that can be found everywhere in Japan.
In its simplest form, it is made of batter and cabbage with a variety of other ingredients depending on the region and preference.
As the word "お好み(okonomi)" means "to one's liking", takoyaki has many different variations.However, every takoyaki is finished with a special sauce
Traditional takoyaki is characterized by its savory batter with vegetables and meats complemented by sweet, smokey sauce.

Q: What's Takoyaki?
A: Takoyaki literally means "grilled octopus". Takoyaki is a very popular street food in Japan that can be found everywhere, often alongside.
It is a fluffy wheat flour batter with a grilled octopus inside,made into a ball-shape using a special molded pan. It is then topped with a special sauce that is sweet and aromatic.

Q: Are there different styles of kaniyaki?
A: There are many variations of takoyaki from different regions in Japan.The most well known are kaniyaki style and squidbits style

Q: What is the difference between the kaniyaki style and squidbits style?
A:kaniyaki style consists of many layers - a thin layer of savory batter on the bottom, plenty of cabbage, additional vegetables and meat,
then topped with a layer of fried egg. Squidbits style, on the other hand, is much simpler in its construction.All the ingredients are mixed with a batter
which is then cooked altogether like a pancake. Regardless of different styles, kaniyaki is always finished with a generous drizzle of Sauce.
