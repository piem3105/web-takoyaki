Refund policy

At Primes takoyaki., we take great pride in the quality of our products and inspect them thoroughly before delivering them to our customers.
If you decide for any reason that you would like to return an item from your order, it must arrive at our store within 30 minutes of purchase. The product must be unopened and in its original packaging. We cannot accept returns of any item that has been opened. Please indicate the order number and the date and time your order was placed on your return. When the item to be returned arrives at our store, we will inspect it to ascertain its quality. You will be refunded the cost of the product minus the delivery cost. Please send returns directly to our main store: Villa de calamba primes store Please feel free to contact us at any time should you have any questions or concerns. Any inquiries may be directed at primes@gmail.com or contact us +639309315661.
